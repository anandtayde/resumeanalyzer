Position: Senior Growth Product Manager
Company: Velocity Pay
Location: New York, NY (Hybrid)

ABOUT THE ROLE
Velocity Pay is a fast-growing FinTech company. We are looking for a Senior Growth Product Manager to own our acquisition and expansion funnel. In this role, you will run rapid experimentation to optimize user sign-up, activation, and monetization. You will collaborate with growth marketing, data science, and engineering to drive growth.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
- Own the conversion, onboarding, and monetization funnels, running continuous A/B testing.
- Design, deploy, and analyze growth experiments using Optimizely, Mixpanel, and SQL.
- Implement Product-Led Growth (PLG) strategies to scale acquisition virally.
- Define pricing and packaging experiments to optimize average revenue per user (ARPU).
- Partner with product marketing to align messaging and launch growth campaigns.

REQUIREMENTS & SKILLS
- 5+ years of Product Management experience, with at least 2 years in a Growth PM role.
- Extensive experience running A/B tests and multivariate experiments.
- Strong analytical skills, with advanced SQL knowledge and experience with Mixpanel, Optimizely, or Heap.
- Deep understanding of FinTech compliance, payment gateways, and subscription billing.
- Experience building financial models or working on SaaS pricing strategies.
- Background in software engineering or a highly technical degree is a strong plus.
